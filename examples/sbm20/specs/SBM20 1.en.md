# Hard Beta and Gamma Radiation Counter SBM-20

Handwritten note: “γ radiation from 10 μR/h to 100 (200) mR/h”

## Technical Specifications ODO.339.172 TU

**Nominal operating voltage:**
400 V

**Length of the plateau of the counting characteristic:**
Not less than 100 V

**Slope of the plateau of the counting characteristic:**
Not more than 0.1% per volt

**Range of registered dose rates of gamma radiation:**
P_min: 0.004 μR/s (14.4 μR/h)
P_max: 40 μR/s (144 mR/h)

**Sensitivity to gamma radiation**
Ra-226 at a dose rate of 0.1 P_max: 420 ± 20 pulses/s

**Intrinsic background:**
No more than 1 pulse/s

**Pulse amplitude:**
Not less than 50 V

**Maximum permissible current:**
20 μA

**Service life not less than:**
2×10¹⁰ pulses

**Operating temperature range:**
–50 °C - +70 °C

The tube operates at a temperature of +85 °C for 125 hours.

Change in the sensitivity of the counter over its full service life does not exceed ±20%, during operation both in pulse and current mode.
