## Specifications

Product model: SBM-20

* Hard beta and gamma radiation counter

Mechanical specifications:

* Total length: 108 ± 3.5 mm
* Total diameter: 10.5 – 11.2 mm
* Effective length: 70 mm
* Effective diameter: 10 ± 1 mm
* Terminal length: ≤ 8.5 mm
* Terminal diameter: 6.3 – 6.5 mm
* Cathode material: stainless steel, 40 mg/cm² (50 µm)
* Cathode glass isolation length: 2 mm
* Cathode glass isolation diameter: 5 mm
* Cathode glass anode holder length: 5 mm
* Cathode glass anode holder diameter: 3.5 mm
* Anode length: 77 mm
* Anode length in effective volume: 66 mm
* Anode diameter: 1.5 mm
* Anode glass isolation length: 3.5 mm
* Anode glass isolation diameter: 10 mm
* Fill gas composition: Ne 99.2 – 99.4%, Ar 0.45 – 0.65%, Br₂ 0.15 – 0.2%
* Fill gas pressure: 240 – 330 hPa (180 – 250 mmHg, 0.2 – 0.34 kg/cm²)
* Operating Temperature Range: -60 °C – +70 °C (125 hours at +85 °C)
* Shelf life: ≥ 4 years
* Weight: ≤ 10 g

Electrical specifications:

* Starting voltage: 260 – 320 V
* Operating voltage: 350 – 475 V
* Recommended operating voltage: 400 V
* Plateau length: ≥ 100 V
* Plateau slope: ≤ 0.1%/V
* Pulse amplitude: ≥ 50 V
* Permissible current: ≤ 20 μA
* Inter-electrode resistance: ≥ 1 GΩ
* Minimum anode resistor: 1.0 MΩ
* Recommended anode resistor: 5.1 MΩ
* Capacitance: ≤ 10.5 pF

Radiological specifications:

* Background level: ≤ 1 count/s
* Measuring range: 0.004 – 40 µR/s (14.4 µR/h – 144 mR/h)
* Gamma sensitivity Ra-226: 29 cps/mR/h (420 ± 20 counts/s at 4 µR/s)
* Gamma sensitivity Cs-137: 18 cps/mR/h (260 ± 20 counts/s at 4 µR/s)
* Sensitivity to beta radiation: ≤ ± 15%
* Sensitivity change over life time: ≤ ± 20%
* Intrinsic background: ≤ 1 count/s
* Pulse amplitude: ≥ 50 V
* Maximum operating count rate: 10 kcounts/s
* Dead time at 400 V: ≤ 190 µs
* Life expectancy: ≥ 2×10¹⁰ pulses
