## General notes / callouts

1. Dimensions for reference.

2. Wire coating No. 4: solder “Прб КР1 ПОС-40 ГОСТ 21931-76”.

3. Evacuate and fill with gas mixture:
   - high-purity argon (0.45–0.6)% ТУ 6-21-12-79;
   - high-purity neon (99.4–99.2)% ТУ 6-21-29-78;
   - Bromine (0.15-0.2)% “ЯГЛК 10330.0007” to a final pressure of P = (2.4–3.33)×10⁴ Pa, (180–250) mmHg, (0.2–0.34) kg/cm².

4. Basic electrical and radiometric characteristics of the counter:

   4.1 Operating voltage range: (350–475) V
   4.2 Counting start voltage: (260–320) V
   4.3 Plateau length of counting characteristic not less than: 100 V
   4.4 Plateau slope of counting characteristic not more than: 0.15%/V

5. Maximum sensitivity to gamma radiation from a Cs-137 source at P = 4 µR/s not less than: (27±1) µR⁻¹

6. Pulse amplitude not less than: 50 V

7. Intrinsic background not more than: 1 count/s

8. Inter-electrode resistance not less than: 1×10⁹ Ω
