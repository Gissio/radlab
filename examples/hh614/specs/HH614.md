## Specifications

Product model: HH614

* Stainless steel cathode, coaxial cylindrical structure
* Stainless steel anode, glass, valve guide wire
* Detecting: gamma radiation

Mechanical specifications:

* Total length: 55 ± 2 mm
* Total diameter: 6.5 ± 0.5 mm
* Terminal interface (flat glass) length: 7 mm
* Effective length: 35 mm
* Effective diameter: 4.3 mm
* Glass thickness: 0.8 mm
* Cathode thickness: 0.1 mm
* Anode diameter: 1 mm
* Lead length: 14 ± 5 mm
* Operating temperature: -50 – +70 °C
* Weight: 1.65 g

Electrical specifications:

* Starting voltage: 280 V – 350 V
* Operating voltage range: 400 – 500 V
* Recommended operating voltage: 420 V
* Maximum voltage: 550 V
* Plateau range: 100 V
* Plateau slope: ≤ 30% / 100 V

Radiological response:

* Background level: 8 cpm
* Measuring range: 0.005 – 20 cGY/h
* Gamma sensitivity (Co-60): 10 cps/mR/h
* Life expectancy: ≥ 10⁹ pulses
* Dead time: ≤ 15 μs

## Measurements (Gissio)

* Dead time (measured): 350 μs
