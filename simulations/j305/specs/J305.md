# J305/J321 Geiger-Müller tube

## Sources

* https://www.schmoozie.co.uk/radmon/info-tubes.php

## Specifications

Product model: J305

* Tin oxide cathode, coaxial cylindrical thin shell structure
* Product material: glass
* Detecting: beta and gamma rays

Mechanical specifications:

* Total length: 107 mm ± 3 mm
* Total diameter: 10 mm ± 1 mm
* Effective length: 82 mm
* Effective diameter: 9 mm
* Terminal length: 9 mm
* Terminal diameter: 6.5 mm
* Wall thickness: 0.5 mm
* Anode thickness: 0.75 mm
* Anode glass holder diameter: 3 mm

Electrical specifications:

* Starting voltage: ≤ 350 V
* Operating voltage: 360 – 440 V
* Recommended operating voltage: 380 V
* Plateau slope: ≤ 12.5% / 100 V

Radiological specifications:

* Background level: 25 cpm
* Measuring range: 0.005 – 20 cGY/h
* Gamma sensitivity (Co-60): 44 cps/mR/h
* Life expectancy: ≥ 10⁹ pulses
* Operating temperature: -40 – +55 °C
