# Counter SBM20U

## Label

The SBM20U counter is intended for registering hard gamma and beta radiation in the dose-rate range up to 40 μR/s in radiotechnical devices.

Climate performance: UHL 2.1

*(Technical drawing with dimensions)*

Weight no more than 10 g.
