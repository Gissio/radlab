## 3. Storage information

The counters must be stored in heated and ventilated warehouses in the supplier’s packaging at temperatures from +5 to +35 °С and relative air humidity up to 80%.

## 4. Warranty obligations

The manufacturing enterprise guarantees that counters **SBM19** meet the requirements of technical specifications OTU 339.027 TU during the entire shelf life and service life within the limits of the shelf life, provided that the consumer complies with the operating modes and conditions, storage rules, and transportation requirements, as well as the instructions for use specified in the technical specifications.

## 5. Information on claims

In case the counters do not meet the requirements of OTU 339.027 TU, they must be returned to the manufacturer together with the passport, indicating the following information:

- storage period
- date of failure
- number of registered pulses
- basic data on operating conditions
- reasons for rejecting the counters
