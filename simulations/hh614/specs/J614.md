## Specifications

Product model: J614

* Product material: glass
* Detecting: gamma rays

Mechanical specifications:

* Total length: 55 mm
* Total diameter: 7.5 mm
* Effective length: 30 mm
* Effective diameter: 3.5 mm

Electrical specifications:

* Starting voltage: 350 V
* Recommended operating voltage: 420 V
* Plateau range: 100 V
* Plateau slope: ≤ 15% / 100 V

Radiological specifications:

* Background level: 10 cpm
* Measuring range: 0.005 – 20 cGY/h
* Gamma sensitivity (Co-60): 5 cps/mR/h
* Operating temperature: -40 – +50 °C
* Life expectancy: ≥ 10¹⁰ pulses
* Dead time: ≤ 15 μs
