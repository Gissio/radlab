# SI-3BG Geiger-Müller tube

## Sources

* https://www.schmoozie.co.uk/radmon/info-tubes.php

## Specifications

Product model: SBM-20

* Hard beta and gamma radiation counter

Mechanical specifications:

* Total length: 55 ± 5 mm
* Total diameter: ≤ 10 mm
* Effective length: 8 mm
* Effective diameter: 2 mm
* Terminal length: 7.1 – 7.9 mm
* Terminal diameter: 6.3 – 6.5 mm
* Anode diameter: 0.5 mm
* Operating Temperature Range: -40 °C – +50 °C
* Shelf life: ≥ 8 years
* Weight: ≤ 10 g

Electrical specifications:

* Starting voltage: 290 – 330 V
* Operating voltage in pulse mode: 380 – 460 V
* Operating voltage in current mode: 382 – 398 V
* Plateau length: ≥ 80 V
* Plateau slope: ≤ 0.25%/V
* Current-voltage slope at 390 ± 8 V: ≤ 1.5%/V
* Current consumption: 15 – 20 μA
* Recommended operating voltage: 400 V
* Recommended anode resistor: 2 – 6.7 MΩ
* Capacitance: ≤ 3 pF

Radiological specifications:

* Sensitivity: 188 – 282 cps/R/h
* Typical sensitivity: 235 cps/R/h
* Maximum working exposure: 300 R/h
* Maximum overload exposure (1 min): 900 R/h
